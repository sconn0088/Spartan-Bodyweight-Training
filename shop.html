Find the right tools and equipment to build your ultimate Spartan workout experience.

Dip Bar Station

In order to perform the dip correctly, you need to suspend yourself in the air and be high enough up that you don't touch the ground when in the lowered position. Two chairs might work, but if you need something taller, the dip bar station could be perfect for you.

Pull Up Bar

There's no other way around it: If you want to do pull ups, you need a bar. Check out this multi-grip monster, capable of supporting up to 300 pounds.

Power Tower

If you have the space and the cash, you might consider investing in the power tower, which combines the best of both worlds: dips and pull ups.

Push Up Bars

While not required to perform push ups, if you're looking for different positions to the standard push up, or perhaps want a wider range of motion for the handstand push up, these bars just might be what the doctor ordered.
